About Me

Engineering Student &
Aspiring Full-Stack Developer

I am a second-year Information Science & Engineering student currently in my 4th semester at the Bangalore Institute of Technology. My journey in tech is driven by a fascination with how code can bridge the gap between complex logic and beautiful, user-centric design.

When I'm not in the classroom, I'm usually found with a crochet hook in hand or a stylus in my art kit.
